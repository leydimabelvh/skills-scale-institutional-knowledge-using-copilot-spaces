# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Test Engineers

### Role Summary
QA/Test Engineers ensure quality standards are met through systematic testing and validation. They collaborate with the team to identify issues early and verify acceptance criteria are satisfied.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans and test cases
- Perform manual and automated testing
- Validate acceptance criteria and Definition of Done
- Report and track defects
- Participate in planning to define testability requirements

### Goals
- Catch issues before they reach production
- Maintain high quality standards across releases
- Improve test coverage and automation

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and backlog refinement
- Daily standups to report blockers and test status
- Bug reports and test reports
- Collaboration with Developers on fixes

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Master facilitates sprint planning, standups, and retrospectives. They remove impediments, coach the team in agile practices, and safeguard delivery cadence.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate scrum ceremonies (standup, planning, retrospective, review)
- Monitor team progress and remove obstacles
- Coach team on Agile values, tools, and processes
- Collaborate with PM to align delivery goals
- Shield the team from external disruptions

### Goals
- Enable team productivity and continuous improvement
- Ensure adherence to agile principles and practices
- Foster a collaborative, transparent work environment

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups (facilitator)
- Sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Impediment tracking and escalation
- Coaching conversations with team members

### Interactions
Coordinates directly with Developers, PM, Product Manager, and QA/Test. Acts as a buffer to external disruptions and facilitates cross-team communication.

---

## Engineering Lead

### Role Summary
Engineering Lead oversees technical delivery, architectural decisions, and cross-team alignment on engineering standards. They bridge technical communication to stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Approve technical design and architecture
- Mentor developers and set coding standards
- Participate in capacity planning and estimation
- Escalate technical risks and dependencies
- Review critical code changes and technical decisions
- Ensure technical debt is managed appropriately

### Goals
- Maintain high engineering quality and standards
- Foster technical growth and knowledge sharing
- Balance technical excellence with delivery speed

### Typical Communication
- Architecture and design review sessions
- Technical risk discussions
- Developer mentoring and 1-on-1s
- Cross-team technical alignment meetings

### Interactions
Works with Developers, PM, Product Manager, QA/Test, and Scrum Master. Bridges technical communication to stakeholders and leadership.

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designer ensures user experience targets are met and final design is both usable and aligned to business goals. They advocate for the end user throughout the product lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Deliver wireframes, mockups, and design assets
- Research user needs and validate usability
- Align product requirements and technical constraints with design
- Collaborate in sprint reviews to demo user-facing changes
- Create and maintain design systems and patterns
- Conduct usability testing and incorporate feedback

### Goals
- Create intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Ensure design consistency across the product
- Validate designs with real user feedback

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and walkthroughs
- Collaboration sessions with Product Manager and Developers
- Usability testing sessions
- Sprint reviews and demos

### Interactions
Works closely with Product Manager, Developers, and QA/Test during planning, execution, and review. Engages with Support/Customer Success for user feedback.

---

## Compliance / Regulatory Stakeholder

### Role Summary
Compliance/Regulatory Stakeholder reviews product or process changes for alignment to legal, regulatory, and company requirements. They ensure the team stays compliant with applicable standards.

### Responsibilities
- Review changes for compliance with regulations and policies
- Flag risks or gaps to project team
- Advise on policy and documentation update needs
- Approve releases that require compliance sign-off
- Monitor ongoing compliance requirements

### Goals
- Prevent compliance violations and legal risks
- Ensure proper documentation and audit trails
- Educate teams on compliance requirements

### Typical Communication
- Compliance reviews at key milestones
- Risk assessments and audit reports
- Policy updates and training sessions
- Release approval communications

### Interactions
Informed by PM and Product Manager during releases or major changes. Works with Engineering Lead and Developers when compliance affects technical implementation.

---

## Support / Customer Success

### Role Summary
Support/Customer Success provides feedback on user issues, collects post-release data, and ensures new features are ready for customer-facing teams. They serve as the voice of the customer.

### Responsibilities
- Communicate customer issues and trends to team
- Validate resolution of critical issues
- Serve as liaison for release documentation and training
- Provide input on feature usability and customer impact
- Track customer satisfaction and adoption metrics

### Goals
- Ensure smooth customer experience with new releases
- Reduce support burden through quality and documentation
- Advocate for customer needs in product decisions

### Typical Communication
- Customer issue escalations and trend reports
- Release readiness reviews
- Documentation and training material feedback
- Customer feedback sessions

### Interactions
Works with PM, QA/Test, Product Manager, and UX Designer to ensure smooth handoff after release. Provides critical customer feedback to inform backlog priorities.
